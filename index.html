About Me

Hi! I'm Linh, a software engineer in the San Francisco Bay Area, originally from SoCal and a recent UCLA graduate (go Bruins!) As a lifelong learner fueled by Vietnamese coffee and curiosity, I am always seeking to expand my knowledge and improve my efficacy as a developer.

My love for coding rivals my obsession with animals, so when I'm not coding you can find me scrolling through my camera roll, admiring my dogs/cats/hamsters/rats/ferret/opossums.

Projects

Zit Zapper

Zit Zapper is a quick game in which you pop as many zits as you can in 30 seconds, with more points being earned for shorter reaction times. I created this game after two weeks of learning to code. I thought it would be a fun way for myself and anyone playing it to destress.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery

Avocados Anonymous

In the fashion of a hackathon, I collaborated with one other developer and two UX designers for less than two days to create an app with the intention of enticing users into subscribing to our newsletter. My focus was on the front and backend of the subscription form itself, involving Bootstrap, form validation, and a success modal.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, Bootstrap, Express.js, Body-parser, MongoDB, Mongoose

Podfeedr

In one week, I worked with one other developer to develop a full-stack app that allows users to store all the podcasts they could think of in one place and select their favorites. We utilized the iTunes API to retrieve podcast information. I focused primarily on user authentication and form validation, as well as design.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Express.js, Body-parser, Bcrypt, MongoDB, Mongoose

Wayfarer

Myself and three other developers built a full-stack app that allows the user to create an account and then write traveler posts for various cities from a database. I alternated between working on the frontend and the backend, contributing primarily to user authentication and post and comment features.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Express.js, Body-parser, Bcrypt, MongoDB, Mongoose, React
